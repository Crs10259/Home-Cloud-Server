About the Project

Home Cloud Server is an open-source personal cloud storage solution designed to give users complete control over their data. Built with security, performance, and ease of use in mind.

Our mission is to provide a reliable alternative to commercial cloud providers while maintaining the highest standards of privacy and data ownership.
